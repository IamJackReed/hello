Based in London and working at Legal & General. I use this site to share my thoughts on teams, products, organisations, and financial markets.

"I like figuring out how value is created - inside organisations and in the markets that price them."

I partner with organisations to connect strategy, product, and execution - and I invest in businesses that do the same internally. I'm interested in how value is created: how teams work together effectively, how products create value, how systems shape behaviour, and how markets eventually price the results.

What I Write About

On this site I write about teams, products, organisations, and financial markets. I explore how incentives and systems shape behaviour in organisations, and occasionally share experiments, mistakes, and lessons. Some of that investment research will appear here — not as financial advice or trade alerts, but as a record of how I'm trying to make sense of businesses and markets.
